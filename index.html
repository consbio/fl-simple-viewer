1 Compare Key Characteristics across Florida

Use the Filter tab above to explore different combinations of key characteristics assessed at the watershed scale across the state.

Simply click on a bar to show only watersheds that have that value for that factor. You can combine filters across factors to see unique combinations, such as the watersheds that have the highest amount of priority resources AND highest biodiversity.

2 Explore Details for a Watershed

To find out more information about a specific watershed, click on it in the map. The Details tab will show a rich collection of descriptive information about that watershed.
